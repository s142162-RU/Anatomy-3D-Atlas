Muscular System

The muscular system allows movement and helps maintain posture.

Main Functions

  • Movement of the body
  • Maintain posture
  • Produce heat

Types of Muscles

  • Skeletal: Voluntary movement
  • Smooth: Internal organs
  • Cardiac: Heart muscle

How Muscles Work

  1. Receive signals from nerves
  2. Contract and relax
  3. Produce movement

Interesting Facts

  • More than 600 muscles in the body
  • The strongest muscle is the jaw muscle
