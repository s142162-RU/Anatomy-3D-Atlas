Nervous System

The nervous system controls and coordinates all body activities by sending signals between different parts of the body.

Main Functions

  • Control body movements
  • Process information
  • Respond to stimuli

Main Parts

  • Brain: Control center of the body
  • Spinal Cord: Connects brain to body
  • Nerves: Carry signals

Types of Nervous System

  • Central Nervous System (CNS)
  • Peripheral Nervous System (PNS)

How It Works

  1. Receive information from senses
  2. Process in brain
  3. Send response to body

Common Diseases

  • Alzheimer’s disease
  • Parkinson’s disease
  • Epilepsy

Interesting Facts

  • The brain has billions of neurons
  • Nerve signals travel very fast
