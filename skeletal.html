Skeletal System

The skeletal system forms the framework of the body and protects internal organs.

Main Functions

  • Support body structure
  • Protect vital organs
  • Enable movement
  • Produce blood cells

Main Parts

  • Skull: Protects the brain
  • Spine: Supports body and protects spinal cord
  • Ribs: Protect heart and lungs
  • Limbs: Help movement

Bone Types

  • Long bones
  • Short bones
  • Flat bones
  • Irregular bones

Interesting Facts

  • Adults have 206 bones
  • Babies are born with more bones
