Circulatory System

The circulatory system transports oxygen, nutrients, and waste products.

Main Functions

  • Transport oxygen
  • Deliver nutrients
  • Remove waste

Main Components

  • Heart: Pumps blood
  • Blood: Carries substances
  • Vessels: Transport blood

Blood Flow Process

  1. Heart pumps blood
  2. Blood travels through vessels
  3. Cells receive oxygen

Interesting Facts

  • The heart beats 100,000 times per day
  • Blood travels thousands of kilometers daily
